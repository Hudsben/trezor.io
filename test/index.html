Our Story

The history of hardware wallets begins with TREZOR.

Early 2013, Alena, Pavol and Marek, the core team and co-founders of SatoshiLabs announced their new project of a bitcoin hardware wallet. What started as a hobby project a few months earlier, resulted in TREZOR, world’s first and most secure hardware wallet.

TREZOR implements original state-of-the-art cryptographic algorithms and security practices to ensure that a user’s bitcoins stay completely secure.

Gradually, TREZOR has introduced new functionalities expanding its benefits to major digital currencies (Bitcoin, Ethereum, Litecoin, Dash, Zcash, Dogecoin, Namecoin), password-less login to websites, 2nd factor authentication (U2F by FIDO), SSH login or even GPG encryption for emails and documents. TREZOR also introduced it's own Password Manager, the first ultimately secure storage for your login credentials online. TREZOR Password Manager completely elimminates the use of a master password, typical security weakness of other password managers.

TREZOR has made it easy for anyone to hold and transact bitcoins with a complete peace of mind. It has returned the bitcoins to the hands of individual users, without having to entrust their money to other companies. TREZOR took away the fear of making mistakes with bitcoin from newbies. That's why TREZOR has practically become synonymous with “hardware wallet” and found its happy users in over 100 countries.
